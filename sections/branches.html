Our Branches

NKCyber is an organization for students who are passionate about computer security. Beyond classroom learning, students get together frequently to learn how malicious attackers utilize software, network vulnerabilities to get into computer networks, how to harden computer systems, dissect malicious code and how to defend against intrusions.

Cyber Security Training

Discover cyber security with our CST program. Led by students, for students, our immersive training includes defense tactics, red team skills, and more. Join us to unveil the exciting realm of cyber security.

Research and Development

Discover the junction of coding and cybersecurity in R&D. Join a close-knit community that sparks innovation through impactful projects. Step into the realm of coding, unlocking your cyber potential.

Build Team

Get hands-on with infrastructure, networking, and DevOps in the Build Team. We keep things running and teach secure server setups. Join our close community for learning and cyber empowerment.
